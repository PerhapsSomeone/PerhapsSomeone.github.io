MTU

MineToUnlock is a way to effectively monetize your website while turning all adblockers useless. Simply embed the below snippet, configure the config tag and you're ready to go!

<script src="https://PerhapsSomeone.github.io/projects/js/mtu.js"></script> <config target="256" key="YDJ7PxO2b2mCgMyXQJDkolqvGWG3JoKx" />

The config tag accepts two parameters, the number of hashes needed to proceed and your CoinBase key. I provided mine for example purposes.

The script will lock the content of your site using a fixed box that overlays it and disallows scrolling.
